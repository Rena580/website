About me

This is my website about a Safety Plan for First Nation Womens. I am Chilcotin(Tsilhqot'in),from Redstone (TsiDeldel) is located 188 km West of Williams Lake, along Highway 20. Tsilhqot'in consists of six bands which are Tl'etinqox (Anaham), Tŝi Deldel (Redstone), Yuneŝit'in Government (Stone), Xeni Gwet'in First Nations Government (Nemiah), ʔEsdilagh (Alexandria) and Tl'esqox (Toosey).
